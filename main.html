Vitor Jones Anicio
Vitor is a Junior at Seven Lakes High School. He has been acting since his 7th grade and has been a part of 11 mainstage productions, 3 years a member of comedysportz, and a Thespian Troupe 7014 Member since his freshman year. He loves acting with all his heart and plans to continue auditioning, performing, and working hard to improve as an actor.
